---
title: How To Unbind A Persistent Volume
date: 2024-08-22
description: PVs set with Retain reclaimPolicy can be released
published: true
tags: ["kubernetes", "storage"]
authors:
  - corey
---

```bash, title="Replace $pvName with the name of your Persistent Volume"
kubectl patch pv $pvName -p '{"spec":{"claimRef": null}}'
```
